## BUSINESS MODEL (READ CAREFULLY)

**We DO NOT clean clothes.**
**We DO NOT own cleaning equipment.**
**We DO NOT operate a cleaning facility.**

We are a **luxury logistics and concierge layer** that:
- Acquires customers at premium prices
- Collects garments from their homes/offices
- Partners with established dry cleaners as our back-end
- Returns garments to customers
- Manages everything else: CX, branding, quality control, subscriptions, payments

**Revenue Sources:**
- Markup on cleaning (we charge £X, pay cleaner £Y, keep the margin)
- Collection fees
- Delivery fees
- Express surcharges
- Monthly/annual membership subscriptions
- Corporate account contracts
- Airbnb/hotel linen service contracts

**Positioning:** "The Harrods of Garment Care" — ultra-premium, white-glove, concierge service.
