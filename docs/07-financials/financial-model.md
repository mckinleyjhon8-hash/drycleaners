## 10. KEY FINANCIAL ASSUMPTIONS

1. Gross margin maintained at 50%+ through supplier negotiation
2. Collection/delivery route efficiency improves monthly (less fuel per order)
3. Corporate accounts won from Month 4 onwards
4. Membership subscriptions built from Month 2 onwards
5. 60-75% customer repeat rate after first order
6. 1 driver van covers 12-15 pickups/day in MK
7. Marketing spend stays below 15% of revenue
8. Stony Dry Cleaners provides wholesale pricing at 35-45% of retail
